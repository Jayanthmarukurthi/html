Nike produces a wide range of sports equipment and apparel. Their first products were track running shoes. Nike Air Max is a line of shoes first released by Nike, Inc. in 1987. Additional product lines were introduced later, such as Air Huarache, which debuted in 1992.The most recent additions to their line are the Nike 6.0, Nike NYX, and Nike SB shoes, designed for skateboarding. Nike's range of products include shoes, jerseys, shorts, cleats, baselayers, etc. for sports activities such as association football, [67] basketball, track and field, combat sports, tennis, American football, athletics, golf, ice hockey, and cross training for men, women, and children. Nike's recent product launch, the Air Max DN, marks a significant moment for the company, as it completely sold out on Nike.com. This new product debut comes at a crucial time when the company has faced criticism over a perceived lack of innovation. The sellout of the Air Max DN aligns with the management's previously the analyst noted that for a company with annual revenues surpassing $50 billion, one successful sneaker launch is not enough to significantly impact the overall business.
